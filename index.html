Tour journey

Day 01 - Srinagar Arrival

On Arrival, meet with our representative & assist at the airport Station and transfer to Hotel in Srinagar. Our night stay will be at the Srinagar Hotel.

Day 02 - Srinagar

After breakfast, proceed to local Srinagar sightseeing of the Mughal Gardens, Asiaâ€™s Largest Tulips Garden, Nishat Bagh built by Asaf Khan in 1632 AD, and Shalimar Bagh built by Jehangir for his wife empress NurJahan. Enjoy a Shikara ride at the lake. Overnight stay in Srinagar Hotel.

Day 03 - Srinagar - Gulmarg

After breakfast, proceed to a full-day tour of Gulmarg, which is one of the most beautiful summer/winter resorts in the Kashmir valley. It is 60 kms away and will be covered in approximately 2 hours. Gulmarg has one of the best ski slopes in the world and the highest golf course with 18 holes. Enjoy the splendor of nature and the snowy mountains, including sledge riding on snow. Late afternoon, drive back to Srinagar for the night in the hotel.

Day 04 - Srinagar - Pahalgam

After breakfast, drive from Gulmarg to Pahalgam (The Valley of Shepherds), which is about 160 kms away and will be covered in approximately 4 hours. You can visit the saffron fields of Pampore, Awantipura Ruins (1100 years old temple of Lord Vishnu built by King Awantivarman) en-route. Drive through the pine forests. Arrive at Pahalgam and enjoy the Lidder stream. Our night will be in Pahalgam.

Day 05 - Srinagar/Sonamarg/Srinagar

Full-day excursion to Sonamarg (Golden Meadow). The distance of about 83 kms will be covered in about 2.5 hours. Sonamarg lies in Sindh Valley, strewn with flowers and surrounded by mountains. It is also the base for some interesting treks to the high altitude of Himalayan Lakes. Enjoy horse riding up to Thajwas Glacier and sledge riding on snow. Late afternoon, drive back to Srinagar for the night in the Srinagar Hotel.

Day 06 - Srinagar - Doodhpathri

After breakfast, drive from Srinagar to Doodhpathri, which lies in a bowl-shaped valley in the Pir Panjal Range of the Himalayas at an altitude of 2730 meters (8957 feet) above sea level. It is an alpine valley covered with snow-capped mountains and meadows of pine, fir, and deodar. The distance of 50 kms will be covered in approximately 1 hour. Late afternoon, drive back to Srinagar for the night in Srinagar Hotel.

Day 07 - Srinagar Drop

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and drive to Srinagar airport, which is 3 kms away and takes about 15 minutes. Drop off at Srinagar airport.

After transfers to your destination flight/bus, the tour ends with unforgettable memories, which we believe will give us a chance to serve you again and again.
